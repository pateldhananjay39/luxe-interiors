FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Luxe Interiors. Can't find your answer here? Send us a message.

Our process begins with a complimentary discovery call to understand your goals and budget. From there, we move into a paid design consultation where we develop a concept direction. Once approved, we create detailed design drawings, source materials and furniture, and manage installation. You'll be involved at every key milestone, with regular updates throughout.

Timelines vary by project scope. A single-room redesign typically takes 6–10 weeks from concept to completion. Full-home projects range from 4 to 12 months, depending on custom fabrication, renovation work, and furniture lead times. We provide a detailed project timeline at the start of every engagement.

We offer three engagement models: a flat design fee for smaller projects, an hourly consultation rate, and a percentage-of-project fee for full-service design. All pricing is discussed transparently during your initial consultation. There are no hidden fees — you always know exactly where your investment is going.

Yes! While our primary studio is in New York, we regularly work with clients across the United States and internationally. We handle remote projects seamlessly using our virtual design process, including 3D renderings and video walkthroughs, with strategic site visits for key milestones.

Absolutely — your involvement is what makes great design possible. We present all concept directions, material boards, and furniture selections to you for approval before proceeding. Our collaborative approach means you're always in control, guided by our expertise.

Yes, our full-service offering includes procurement, logistics management, and installation. We have access to trade-only vendors and showrooms, which means our clients receive exceptional quality at prices not available to the general public. We manage everything from delivery to final styling.

We don't believe in a single signature style — we believe in finding yours. Our team has expertise across contemporary, transitional, modern classic, Scandinavian, and maximalist aesthetics. We've designed everything from sleek minimalist apartments to richly layered traditional homes. What unifies all our work is a commitment to timeless quality and thoughtful curation.
